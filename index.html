Stable Currency

OERO converts cash into digital currency, to anchor the value to the price of national EURO currencies.

100% Backed

Every OERO is always 100% backed by our reserves, which include traditional currency and cash equivalents and, from time to time, may include other assets and receivables from loans made by OERO to third parties, which may include affiliated entities (collectively, “reserves”). Every OERO is also 1-to-1 pegged to the euro, so 1 OERO token is always valued at 1 euro.

Transparent

The value of our reserves is published daily. The value of our reserves matches or exceeds the value of all oeros in circulation.

Blockchain Technology

OERO token is an ERC-20 token. Ethereum based platform is built on top of open blockchain technologies, leveraging the best security and transparency that they provide.

Accessibility for everyone

The key advantage of OERO in loyalty and individual approach to each user or holder of OERO. In whatever country and jurisdiction you would be in.

Secure
